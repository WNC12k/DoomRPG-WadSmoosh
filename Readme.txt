WadSmoosh has been adapted for use in conjunction with Doom RPG rebalance.

If you enable it, the following happens: 

In the transport menu in the Outpost, you can switch between the original parts of Doom:

  – Doom;
  – Doom 2;
  – Master Levels;
  – No Rest For The Living;
  – Plutonia;
  – TNT.
  
  Enjoy!
